async Sys_getRTC_async()

Get date and time to connected WPC device.

str_str
Date and time represented by “A-B-C, D:E:F”.

  • “A” is year.

  • “B” is month.

  • “C” is day.

  • “D” is hour.

  • “E” is minute.

  • “F” is second.

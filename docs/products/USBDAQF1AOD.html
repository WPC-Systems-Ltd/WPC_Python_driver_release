async sys_getRTC()

Get date and time to connected WPC device.

Returns
str_str

Date and time represented by “A-B-C, D:E:F”, where

  • “A” is year

  • “B” is month

  • “C” is day

  • “D” is hour

  • “E” is minute

  • “F” is second
